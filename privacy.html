MindUnplugged — Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE, e.g. May 3, 2026]

MindUnplugged ("the app", "we") is a wellness app focused on digital detox. We designed it to collect as little information as possible.

Who we are

MindUnplugged is an independent project built by [YOUR NAME], a high school student. You can contact us at [YOUR_EMAIL].

What we collect

  • Anonymous account ID. When you open the app for the first time, Firebase creates a random anonymous identifier. We never ask for your name, email, phone, or location.
  • Self-reported wellness data. Mood check-ins, detox sessions, gratitude entries, urge-surf events, and triggers you log. This is stored in Google Firestore under your anonymous ID.
  • App preferences. Theme, notification settings, and 7-Day Diet progress, stored on your device.
  • Crash data (opt-in). If you allow analytics, we collect anonymized crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or address.
  • No location data.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party trackers or ad networks.
  • No microphone, camera, or contacts access.

How we use it

Your data is used only to power features inside the app — your Insights screen, streak, and history. We do not sell, share, or transfer it to any third party for marketing.

Your rights

  • Export your data. Settings → "Export all data" gives you a JSON or CSV copy of everything we have.
  • Delete your data. Settings → "Delete all my data" permanently wipes your Firestore record and local app state. There is no recovery.

Children's privacy

MindUnplugged is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Because the app does not collect names or contact information, we have no way to contact a parent or guardian. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, contact us and we will delete any data associated with the anonymous ID.

Data location & retention

Data is stored on Google Firebase servers (United States). It is retained until you delete it via the app or stop using the app. Anonymous accounts that are inactive for 24+ months may be removed automatically.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and notify users in-app at next launch.
